# About GlamRock

GlamRock is a fork of [Rockstar](https://codewithrockstar.com), the esoteric programming language created by [Dylan Beattie](https://dylanbeattie.net/) in 2018. Rockstar's syntax is inspired by the lyrics to 1980s hard rock and heavy metal songs.

GlamRock extends Rockstar with practical language features — modules, interop, and more — while keeping the golden rule: **if you can't sing it, you can't have it.**

## Acknowledgements

GlamRock would not exist without the brilliant and absurd work of Dylan Beattie, who created the Rockstar language, the Starship interpreter, and the entire ecosystem around it. GlamRock is built on top of that foundation with gratitude and respect.

Features marked with 💎 in the docs are GlamRock extensions — they are not part of standard Rockstar.
